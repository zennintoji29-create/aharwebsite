Our Culinary Heritage

Born in the heart of Bhubaneswar, Aahar began with a simple mission: to bring the warmth of a mother's kitchen to the city. Every spice is hand-pounded, and every recipe is a family secret passed down through generations. We believe that food isn't just sustenance—it's a celebration of culture and community.

What we're cooking today

Everything we make is traditional and prepared fresh to your order.

Dalma Special ଦାଲ୍ମା ବିଶେଷ

Odisha's oldest comfort food. Born in temple kitchens and perfected in Odia homes — toor dal simmered with chunks of raw banana, yam, green papaya, and brinjal, tempered with panch phoron, dried red chilli, and a slow pour of ghee.

₹180 Pure Veg

Dhenkanal Mutton ଢେଙ୍କାନାଳ ମଟନ

From the hills of central Odisha. In the forested hills of Dhenkanal, mutton is never rushed. Goat pieces are slow-braised in mustard oil with whole spices until the gravy turns the colour of old terracotta and the meat falls off the bone.

₹450 Spicy

Chhena Besara ଛେନା ବେସରା

Mustard is Odisha's soul spice. Fresh chhena cubes bathed in a sharp mustard-poppy paste sharpened with kalonji and dried red chilli — a dish that bites back, in the best Odia way. Pungent and entirely addictive.

₹320 Pure Veg

Chilika Prawns ଚିଲିକା ଚିଙ୍ଗୁଡ଼ି

From Asia's largest coastal lagoon. Plump, sweet, and firm prawns marinated in mustard paste, turmeric, and green chilli, then fried until they blister gold. The sea in every bite.

₹480 Non-Veg

Vegetable Samosa ଶାଗ ସମୋସା

Best eaten in the rain. Hand-folded pastry stuffed with spiced potatoes and local green peas, fried till the shell shatters. Served with our signature green chutney and imli.

₹60 Pure Veg

Gupchup Platter ଗୁପ୍ଚୁପ

Bhubaneswar's evening ritual. Hollow puris that shatter with tangy, cumin-sharp, lip-numbing tamarind water. Filled with spiced yellow peas and mashed potato.

₹120 Spicy

Chakuli Pitha ଚକୁଳି ପିଠା

Odisha's most-loved breakfast. Fermented overnight, poured thin, and crisped at the edges — the Odia answer to the dosa. Served with coconut chutney or tomato achar.

₹80 Pure Veg

Bara Ghuguni ବଡ଼ା ଘୁଗୁନି

The street snack Odisha swears by. Crisp urad dal baras served beside a steaming bowl of yellow pea ghuguni spiced with ginger and green chilli. Eat immediately with lime.

₹90 Pure Veg

Atta Paratha ଆଟା ପରାଠା

Ghee makes it honest. Whole wheat dough rolled to layers and cooked on a cast iron tawa with a generous hand of desi ghee. Simple, honest, and comforting.

₹40 Pure Veg

Pahala Rasagola ପାହାଳ ରସଗୋଲା

Odisha's GI-tagged pride. Soft, cloud-like chhena balls from the village of Pahala that collapse gently in a light sugar syrup. Pure heritage in every bite.

₹40 Sweet

Chhena Poda ଛେନା ପୋଡ

Odisha's answer to crème brûlée. Poda means burnt — chhena mixed with sugar and cardamom, slow-roasted until a deep caramel crust forms on the outside.

₹60 Sweet

Kheeri ଖୀରୀ

Temple prasad turned dessert. Rice pudding dissolved slowly into full-fat milk, perfumed with green cardamom and a pinch of saffron. The perfect end to a meal.

₹70 Sweet
